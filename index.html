INTRO
What I am all about.
Dawei Gao is a Doctor candidate in the State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment (SKLSDE) of the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University (BUAA). He received a bachelor's degree in Computing Science and Engineering from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA). His research interests include Crowdsourcing, Spatio-temporal Data Processing and Analysis, and Machine Learning. He got WAIM 2016 Best Paper Award. His current research focuses on the foundations of advanced data analytics systems that help make the process of building and deploying ML/AI-powered data analytics applications easier and faster. Thus, the key themes of his research are usability, developability, performance, and scalability.
